We're excited to introduce Thunderbolt — an AI assistant built on a simple principle: **you should control your AI tools, not the other way around.**

## Why Thunderbolt?

The AI landscape is full of walled gardens. Your conversations are locked into one provider. Your data lives on someone else's servers. Switching costs are high by design.

Thunderbolt takes a different approach:

- **Choose your models** — Use OpenAI, Anthropic, local models, or any combination
- **Own your data** — Everything syncs to your own infrastructure
- **No lock-in** — Switch providers anytime without losing context

## Built for Teams

With our enterprise offering, organizations can self-host Thunderbolt on their own infrastructure. Full control over data residency, model selection, and access management.
