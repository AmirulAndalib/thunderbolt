Thunderbolt is an AI assistant that puts you in control. Choose your models, own your data, and eliminate vendor lock-in.

## Key Features

- **Model Freedom** — Use any AI model from any provider
- **Data Ownership** — Your conversations and data stay yours
- **No Vendor Lock-in** — Switch providers at any time
- **Self-Hostable** — Deploy on your own infrastructure with our enterprise offering

## Architecture

Thunderbolt is built as a modern web application with offline-first sync powered by PowerSync, a backend API, and a flexible frontend.
